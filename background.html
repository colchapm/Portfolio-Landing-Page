my background

As I briefly touched on in my about me page, I graduated from Indiana University with a major in Sports Marketing and Management and minors in Business and the Japanese Language. For the past seven years I have been working on the Planning and Supply Chain team of an aerospace manufacturer, Precision Castparts Corporation. My work experience and skillset mirrors that of an analyst. I truly enjoy the power of data - how it drives behavior and how it can impact change. While I have moved on to more large-scale responsibilities at Precision Castparts, I continue to use data as the foundation of my work.

Over the past 16 months I have become more and more disconnected with the culture and product of my company. As a result, a lot of those soul-searching questions that we often ask ourselves during times of uncertainty have started surfacing. This reflection has led me taking the Intro to Programming class at Epicodus. I have always been intrigued by back-end programming. Although I don't understand it, I find it fascinating how it makes things work. I am hoping this class will give me more exposure to it and ultimately help me decide if programming could be a possible career change.
